# Facilities (Descriptive)

Facilities are economic/industrial/scientific "activity" units in the simulation. They can represent huge entities such as all of USA on Earth, or small specific entities such as a space agency's specific interest in (or part of) the International Space Station.

In the simulation, there is exactly 1 facility for each player present at each body. Players are defined in `players.tsv`. "Body" is any freely orbiting object, such as a planet, moon, asteroid, or spacecraft. A "facility" really represents all of a player's economic, industrial, and scientific activity at a body.
